Click train detector

Click train detector#

In the following we try to develop a click train detector by means of a detection to click association algorithm

Consider Inter-click-interval (ICI)

(96)#\[\begin{equation} (ICI)_n = t_n - t_{n-1} \end{equation}\]

Assume variation in ICI and the relative change in Power are stochastic processes that vary only randomly

(97)#\[\begin{equation} \Delta (ICI)_n = \Delta (ICI)_{n-1} + N(0,\sigma_C) \end{equation}\]
(98)#\[\begin{equation} \Delta^2 P_n = \Delta^2 P_{n-1} + N(0,\sigma_P) \end{equation}\]

That is

(99)#\[\begin{equation} (ICI)_n = 2 (ICI)_{n-1} - (ICI)_{n-2} + N(0,\sigma_C) \end{equation}\]
(100)#\[\begin{equation} P_n-2P_{n-1}+P_{n-2}=P_{n-1}-2P_{n-2}+P_{n-3}+N(0,\sigma_P) \end{equation}\]

Or equivalently

(101)#\[\begin{equation} t_n=3t_{n-1}-3t_{n-2}+t_{n-3}+N(0,σ_C) \end{equation}\]
(102)#\[\begin{equation} P_n=3P_{n-1}-3P_{n-2}+P_{n-3}+N(0,σ_P) \end{equation}\]

Assume further that boundary reflection (multipath) is is deterimind by the changing geometry

(103)#\[\begin{equation} δτ_n=t_{r,n}-t_n \end{equation}\]

is slowly varying

(104)#\[\begin{equation} Δδτ_n=Δδτ_{n-1}+N(0,σ_τ) \end{equation}\]
(105)#\[\begin{equation} δτ_n = 2 δτ_{n-1}-δτ_{n-2} +N(0,σ_τ) \end{equation}\]

or

(106)#\[\begin{equation} t_{r,n} = t_n+2δτ_{n-1}-δτ_{n-2} +N(0,σ_τ) \end{equation}\]

This would indicate that the click time, received level and surface reflection delay can be considered AR processes.

In summary:

(107)#\[\begin{equation} (ICI)_n = 2 (ICI)_{n-1} - (ICI)_{n-2} + N(0,\sigma_C) \end{equation}\]
(108)#\[\begin{equation} P_n=3P_{n-1}-3P_{n-2}+P_{n-3}+N(0,σ_P) \end{equation}\]
(109)#\[\begin{equation} δτ_n = 2 δτ_{n-1}-δτ_{n-2} +N(0,σ_τ) \end{equation}\]
